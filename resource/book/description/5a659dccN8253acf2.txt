"Silent Confessions" is a long-form debut written by Wuqishi in six years. The story is beautifully arranged, and the writing is calm and introverted. Once published, it has been widely acclaimed and has become a dark horse of the 2014 year. It has not only become a bestseller for the New York Times. It also won the 2014 book selected by numerous media, including major websites of the United States.
