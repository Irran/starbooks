"Besieged City" is a novel written by Qian Zhongshu and is a classic of modern and contemporary Chinese novels. The novel shaped the group image of intellectuals in the early period of the Anti-Japanese War, vividly reflecting the behavior and confusion of special groups in specific periods of the country. From another perspective, the scene and atmosphere at that time were described. Although there is a specific historical background, this novel reveals only the weaknesses of the crowd and can still resonate with people today. The first edition was published by Shanghai Chenguang Publishing Company in 1947. "Besieged City" is a unique satirical novel in the history of modern Chinese literature. Known as "the history of the new Confucianism."
