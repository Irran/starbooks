Knowledge is a network of interrelated concepts through relationships. Learning is to increase understanding of the network, so as to better organize the network, and even promote basic and meaningful creation, and the core of all this is related thinking. Learn, Create, and Use Knowledge Conceptual Diagram for Learning Change in Business and Schools. Discuss what is thinking about relevance, how to develop it through concept maps, why we need it, and how to apply it. This is a book worth reading by all business managers, education administrators, teachers, parents, and students. It is a book that really thinks and partially solves what is taught, how to teach, and why.
