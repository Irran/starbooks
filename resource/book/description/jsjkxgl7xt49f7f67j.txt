"Introduction to Computer Science(5th Edition of English Version)" was written by the highly acclaimed and experienced educators Dale and Lewis in the current domain. It comprehensively introduces the basic knowledge of the field of computer science and outlines a vivid picture for the students of Guangzhou University.. As a whole, the contents of the book are realistic and cover a wide range, aiming to show readers the whole picture of computer science; From the point of view of detail, the level of this book is clear and vivid. Based on the onion structure of the computer system, the information layer, hardware layer, program design layer, operating system layer, application program layer, and communication layer are introduced, involving all aspects of computer science.
